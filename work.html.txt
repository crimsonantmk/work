<header>
  <h1>3-DAY<br>PROGRAM</h1>
  <p>Full body · 3 days per week · No complex movements</p>
</header>

<div class="days">

  <!-- DAY 1 -->
  <div class="day-card day-1">
    <div class="day-header">
      <span class="day-badge">DAY 01</span>
      <span class="day-title">LEG DAY</span>
      <span class="day-focus">Lower Body Focus</span>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
      <div class="section-label">Warm-Up — 5 min</div>
      <div class="exercises">
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Bodyweight Squats</div><div class="ex-note">Light, just to get blood moving</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">2 × 15</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Leg Swings</div><div class="ex-note">Forward + lateral, 10 each side</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">1 × 20</div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
      <div class="section-label">Main Work — Legs</div>
      <div class="exercises">
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Barbell Back Squat</div><div class="ex-note">Feet shoulder-width, chest up, drive through heels</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">4 × 8–10</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Romanian Deadlift</div><div class="ex-note">Hinge at hips, soft knee, bar close to legs</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">3 × 10–12</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Walking Lunges</div><div class="ex-note">Long stride, back knee near floor</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">3 × 12 each</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Leg Press</div><div class="ex-note">Feet hip-width, don't lock knees at top</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">3 × 12–15</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Standing Calf Raise</div><div class="ex-note">Full range — stretch at bottom, squeeze at top</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">4 × 15–20</div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
      <div class="section-label">Full-Body Finisher</div>
      <div class="exercises">
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Plank</div><div class="ex-note">Glutes tight, don't sag the hips</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">3 × 45 sec</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Dumbbell Shoulder Press</div><div class="ex-note">Seated or standing, controlled tempo</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">2 × 12</div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="tip-box">
      <strong>Tip:</strong> Rest 90–120 sec between squat/deadlift sets. 60 sec for everything else. Prioritize depth on squats over load.
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- DAY 2 -->
  <div class="day-card day-2">
    <div class="day-header">
      <span class="day-badge">DAY 02</span>
      <span class="day-title">ARM DAY</span>
      <span class="day-focus">Biceps · Triceps · Shoulders</span>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
      <div class="section-label">Warm-Up — 5 min</div>
      <div class="exercises">
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Arm Circles</div><div class="ex-note">Small to large, both directions</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">2 × 30 sec</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Band Pull-Aparts</div><div class="ex-note">Or towel stretch if no band</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">2 × 15</div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
      <div class="section-label">Main Work — Arms & Shoulders</div>
      <div class="exercises">
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Barbell or EZ-Bar Curl</div><div class="ex-note">Elbows pinned to sides, no swinging</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">4 × 10–12</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Overhead Tricep Extension</div><div class="ex-note">Dumbbell or cable, keep elbows tight</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">4 × 10–12</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Hammer Curl</div><div class="ex-note">Neutral grip, targets brachialis for arm thickness</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">3 × 12</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Tricep Pushdown (cable or band)</div><div class="ex-note">Full extension at bottom, squeeze hard</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">3 × 12–15</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Lateral Raises</div><div class="ex-note">Light weight, slight forward lean, lead with elbow</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">4 × 15</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Face Pulls</div><div class="ex-note">Cable at face height, pull to forehead, elbows high</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">3 × 15</div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
      <div class="section-label">Full-Body Finisher</div>
      <div class="exercises">
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Goblet Squat</div><div class="ex-note">Dumbbell at chest, upright torso</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">2 × 15</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Ab Crunch or Leg Raise</div><div class="ex-note">Slow and controlled, no momentum</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">3 × 15</div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="tip-box">
      <strong>Tip:</strong> Superset curls + tricep work (back to back, short rest) to save time and increase pump. Keep weights moderate — arms respond better to feel than load.
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- DAY 3 -->
  <div class="day-card day-3">
    <div class="day-header">
      <span class="day-badge">DAY 03</span>
      <span class="day-title">CHEST & BACK</span>
      <span class="day-focus">Push · Pull Balance</span>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
      <div class="section-label">Warm-Up — 5 min</div>
      <div class="exercises">
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Push-Ups</div><div class="ex-note">Easy pace, open the chest</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">2 × 10</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Cat-Cow + Thoracic Rotation</div><div class="ex-note">Mobilize the spine before loading it</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">2 × 10</div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
      <div class="section-label">Main Work — Chest & Back</div>
      <div class="exercises">
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Barbell Bench Press</div><div class="ex-note">Medium grip, touch chest, don't bounce</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">4 × 8–10</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Bent-Over Barbell Row</div><div class="ex-note">Hinge 45°, pull to lower chest, squeeze shoulder blades</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">4 × 8–10</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Incline Dumbbell Press</div><div class="ex-note">30–45° bench, targets upper chest</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">3 × 10–12</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Lat Pulldown</div><div class="ex-note">Pull to upper chest, lean back slightly, full stretch at top</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">3 × 10–12</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Cable Chest Fly or Dumbbell Fly</div><div class="ex-note">Wide arc, slight bend in elbows, feel the stretch</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">3 × 12–15</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Seated Cable Row</div><div class="ex-note">Chest up, elbows close to body, full contraction</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">3 × 12</div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="section">
      <div class="section-label">Full-Body Finisher</div>
      <div class="exercises">
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Romanian Deadlift</div><div class="ex-note">Light carry-over from leg day, keeps posterior chain active</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">2 × 12</div>
        </div>
        <div class="exercise">
          <div><div class="ex-name">Dead Bug</div><div class="ex-note">Core anti-extension — opposite arm/leg, slow</div></div>
          <div class="ex-sets">3 × 10 each</div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="tip-box">
      <strong>Tip:</strong> Pair bench press with barbell rows (alternating sets). This saves time and keeps the chest/back balanced so your posture doesn't suffer over time.
    </div>
  </div>

</div>
